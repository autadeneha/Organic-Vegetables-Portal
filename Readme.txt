# Organic Vegetables Portal

## Overview

Organic Vegetables Portal is a full-stack web application developed to connect farmers directly with consumers through an online marketplace. The platform enables users to browse, purchase, and manage organic vegetable products while allowing farmers to efficiently maintain their inventory and orders.

The application aims to promote sustainable agriculture by eliminating unnecessary intermediaries and providing customers with access to fresh, healthy, and organically grown produce.



## Problem Statement

Traditional vegetable supply chains involve multiple intermediaries, resulting in increased prices for customers and reduced profits for farmers. Additionally, consumers often struggle to verify the authenticity and freshness of organic products.

This project provides a centralized online platform where farmers can directly sell their organic vegetables, ensuring transparency, affordability, and efficient order management.

## Project Workflow

1. User registers or logs into the portal.
2. Farmers upload available vegetable products.
3. Customers browse and search products.
4. Selected items are added to the shopping cart.
5. Customers place orders.
6. Farmers process and manage incoming orders.
7. Database updates inventory and order status.
